# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Mason Organizer and TripZero Labs projects to be welcoming, respectful, and focused on learning, building, and improving software together.

Everyone participating in this project is expected to communicate respectfully and professionally.

---

## Expected Behavior

Examples of positive behavior include:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Giving helpful feedback
- Accepting different experience levels
- Keeping discussions focused on the project
- Helping others learn when possible

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Insults, threats, or abusive language
- Discriminatory comments
- Publishing someone else's private information
- Repeatedly derailing project discussions

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or block contributors who violate these expectations.

The goal is to keep Mason Organizer a safe, practical, and productive project for everyone involved.
